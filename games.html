Platform Hero

Platform Hero is a collaborative project done with SJSU comp sci major Nicholas Burnam. This 2D platformer game involes mutiple levels that each offer a different challenge. As the artist of this project, I was in charge of animations, sprites, backgrounds, scenes, and sound design. Platform Hero provides the player with a nostalgic gameplay experience that allows them to become a courageous hero.

Splash

Splash is my first solo video game project. The goal of this project was to recreate the exerience of taking care of a pet fish without the risk that comes with having a live animal. This inerface includes multiple scenes show your pet fish doing different actions. These animations were creatind using JavaScript. I was able to experience taking on the role of a programmer and artist in a single project.
